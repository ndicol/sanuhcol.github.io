I am Columbus

My name is Columbus and Software Development is my ultimate goal

I have personally designed and implemented this webpage to introduce myself and to showcase some simple projects I have built.

Over the past ten years I have gained commercial experiences in various sectors: as an AutoCAD drafting operator for two years and subsequently as an owner/manager of a mini-market/computer repair service for 5 years that was commended by Corriere delle Alpi (a local Newspaper) in 2012 as a success story.

Because of my passion for software, I decided to enrol at The Open University (OU) in 2013 and will be graduating in 2017 with a BSc (Hons) in Computing, IT and Business Management.

I have gained extensive software development knowledge such as agile development, iterative and incremental development, OOP, UML, Java and general systems development life cycle from my studies at the university and I am currently improving my practical skills from lessons and challenges at Free-Code-Camp in technologies and libraries such as HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.

I have worked on some simple JavaScript, Bootstrap, CSS and JQuery projects - some of which could be found on the project section of this page

I welcome commercial Internships/Placements/Graduate Schemes/Entry Level in Software Development, or just feedback, suggestions and bug reporting about this site.
